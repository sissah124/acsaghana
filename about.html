About African Child Sports Academy

Building a brighter future through football, discipline, and education in Ghana.

Who We Are

African Child Sports Academy (ACSA) is a youth-focused football development center based in Accra, Ghana. We identify and nurture raw football talent while instilling academic excellence and character development.

We train young football talents to perfect their skills and market them to academies, schools , agencies and Clubs all over the world.

Our Core Pillars

Our Mission

Empowering African youth through football and holistic education while cultivating discipline, leadership, and ambition.

Our Vision

To become the leading football academy in West Africa, producing well-rounded talent that excels both on and off the pitch.

Our Values

  • Discipline
  • Teamwork
  • Excellence
  • Respect
  • Integrity
